# EDITING NOTE
If the pilot feels too still or too repetitive:
- shorten repeated interview sections first
- keep classroom images working as triggers and resets
- do not be afraid to reuse the strongest stills if the audio underneath is changing the meaning

The audience will forgive repeated images faster than they forgive weak rhythm.
